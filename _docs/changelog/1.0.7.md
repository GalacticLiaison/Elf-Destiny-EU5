# 1.0.7
Sources of Religious Influence have been nerfed across the board

All Elven locations now will desire 2 Calalquendi per 1,000 pop

Calalquendi Estate buildings 
- now give modifiers unique to each building type
- employment 100 => 30 calaquendi

Changed Sanctum building modifiers

Personal Dominions Estate Privilege
- changed calaquendi desired pop modifier to 1 per 1000 pop bonus

Reduced secure flank bonus of Army Magi type units
Calaquendi High Magi
- assigned them a bombard_efficiency stat of 0.25, without the stat I believe they would have been hitting at 100% of bombard phases
- further nerfed some of their defensive stats

Reduced amount of Mythril needed to create Mythril Elven crafts and added a few different production methods

Now during the Story event chain, when deciding to start a new Elven culture from your existing culture, you should unlock all the elven features.
